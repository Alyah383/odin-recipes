Cat Sandwich

A healthy dish for anyone on the go

A delicious and not to mention HIGHLY NUTRITIOUS cat sandwich. It is high in protein which makes it excellent for those looking to bulk

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Cat/Kitten (the bigger the kitty the more protein(while wild cats such as lions or tigers may not only give massive protein boosts but also replenish hp utmost care is required when aquiring them for meals) ))

Steps

  1. Aquire two pieces of bread
  2. Find a cat of some sort and place between the bread
  3. Toppings of your choice can be added for extra flavour
